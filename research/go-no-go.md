# Go / No-Go

Status: `Conditional Go`

## Decision

- Proceed with YouTube Study Copilot as the first product in the portfolio.

## Why

- demand is proven by large transcript and summary extensions
- the learning-retention angle is differentiated enough to justify a focused launch
- policy risk is manageable with the current permission scope and local-first design
- the current MVP is already close to a launch candidate

## Conditions Before Scaling

- replace all placeholder billing, support, and public URL values
- enable and validate telemetry without collecting transcript contents
- verify Chrome Web Store copy positions the product as a study tool, not a vague AI assistant
- validate whether lifetime pricing or subscription pricing converts better
