| Transcript YouTube | https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/transcript-youtube/obigemjopcmecfaacinededdfdcinikk | Established utility-category competitor | Utility-led pricing / mixed category | Strong transcript-only utility framing | Less differentiated as a study and retention product |

## Conclusions

- the category is proven
- transcript demand is larger than flashcard demand today
- the best opening is a lower-friction study tool, not a heavy learning platform on day one
