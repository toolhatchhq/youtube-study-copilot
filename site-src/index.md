# YouTube Study Copilot

Turn captioned YouTube videos into notes, flashcards, quiz prompts, and exports without leaving Chrome.

## Why People Use It

- Turn long educational videos into a quick review pack.
- Save the latest study packs locally in Chrome.
- Export Markdown for free and unlock CSV, JSON, and transcript export with Pro.

## What Is Included

- YouTube caption loading
- AI-powered or fallback study-pack generation
- Local saved-pack library
- Free and Pro plan gating
- Public support, privacy, terms, and changelog pages
